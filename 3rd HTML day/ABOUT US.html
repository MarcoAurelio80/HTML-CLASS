Who are we at MyVikingCycleShop

We are a medium size manufacturing and distribution bicylcle shop. The shop was founded in 2005, in Málaga, Spain, and we have 38 employees. The shop has sponsored over 531 cycling events, including "The Redbull Worlwide Downhill Race" 2020 and 2022, and we have our own racing team since 2015.
